\section*{Code and Data}
\begin{itemize}[label=\textendash,leftmargin=*,topsep=2pt]
  \item \textbf{Code repository:} the full evaluation harness (the three-option choice
    runner, the greedy- and sampled-thinking readouts, and the forking-paths analysis)
    is released at
    \url{https://github.com/unrulyabstractions/apart-june2026}.
  \item \textbf{Data:} the \textsc{SESGO}-derived prompts and per-model outputs are on
    HuggingFace at
    \url{https://huggingface.co/datasets/unrulyabstractions/apart_global_south};
    \textsc{SESGO}~\citep{robles2025sesgo} is used under its original license.
  \item \textbf{Models:} the cross-model sweep covers \texttt{Qwen3.5}
    ($0.8$--$27$B, thinking and non-thinking), \texttt{Gemma 4} (E2B / E4B),
    \texttt{Ministral-3} ($3$B / $8$B, Instruct and Reasoning), and \texttt{Llama-3.1/3.2}
    ($1$B / $3$B / $8$B); the forking-paths dynamics use the \texttt{Qwen3.5} thinking
    models ($0.8$--$9$B).
\end{itemize}
