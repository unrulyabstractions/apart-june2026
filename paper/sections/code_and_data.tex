\section*{Code and Data}
\begin{itemize}[label=\textendash,leftmargin=*,topsep=2pt]
  \item \textbf{Code repository:} the full evaluation harness (the three- and
    two-option choice runners, the greedy- and sampled-thinking readouts, and the
    forking-paths analysis) is released at
    \url{https://github.com/unrulyabstractions/apart-june2026}.
  \item \textbf{Data:} the \textsc{SESGO}-derived prompts and per-model outputs are on
    HuggingFace at
    \url{https://huggingface.co/datasets/unrulyabstractions/apart_global_south};
    \textsc{SESGO}~\citep{robles2025sesgo} is used under its original license.
  \item \textbf{Models:} open-weight Qwen3 (0.6B / 1.7B / 4B / 14B / 32B), Llama-3.2
    (1B / 3B), Llama-3.1 (8B / 70B), Mistral (7B / 24B), and Gemma-2 (2B / 9B).
\end{itemize}
